ABOUT GIXXO

The Gixxo Platform

Gizzo is a next generation system powered by blockchain technology for decentralization by introducing independency of various systems. The name, Gizzo, means spider web in the West African Hausa Language. This highlights the very idea behind the token. It will focus on decentralization of network resources for ultimate delivery of various systems. It is being developed as a decentralized network of multiple applications with the token as the unifying epicenter. The Gizzo team will build a DAPP system with an efficient, equalized and secure network for a safe and reliable delivery of service. In the initial stage, Gizzo team will concentrate on the development of its gaming platform which has an African Mythology theme. At a later stage, it will also launch its ride-hailing platform and delve into NFT exchanges.

The Gizzo Platform provides the following features:

  • In-game token for a mythological themed game
  • Currency for NFT and art transactions
  • Reward for Ride-hailing drivers and riders
  • Provides transparency and trust.

Token Sale & Allocation
