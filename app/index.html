Our Mission

Gizzo’s mission is to place the value of online and offline interactions
into the hands of users. It seeks to incorporate the reward for online
gaming and offline work into a single, accessible token by using blockchain
to increase transparency, speed of earning and automation.
Transparency, quick transactions, easy allocation of earnings and cross chain operability
are essential portions of the Gizzo Platform, as well as a marketplace
that allows the exchange of NFTs to a global community
without the hassles of middlemen.
Gizzo aspires to be as decentralized as possible and aims to be community
driven as it is created with the community and from the community

Why Choose Gixxo

Gizzo’s desire is to provide a platform with an interconnectivity of activities for easy exchange of value as well as fee transparency

Avenue for Artists

Only a truly independent avenue for exchange of work will allow artists and creatives get real value for their works.

Removal of Cost Layers

The Gizzo Platform will remove multiple cost layers involving middlemen, replacing everything with automated transfers between viable individuals.

Transparency

Complete transparency is essential especially when it comes to investments and cryptocurrencies.
